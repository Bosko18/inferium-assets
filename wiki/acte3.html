ACT III · THE DEPTHS

DEPTHS AND DREAD

What sleeps below does not sleep forever.

Below the surface, the Deep Dark and Alex's caves hide ancient horrors.

TOACT III
ACT III · BOSS

The Orc Chief

WhereThe great fortified orc base, in the plains. The small camps are for training.

Fight450 HP, hits for 17. Surrounded by warriors, archers, warlocks. Pull them in small groups. Watch out for Ogres and Minotaurs.

LootChests and orc gear.

The six underground worlds of Alex's Caves are hidden very deep.

Craft the Cave Biome Map: it guides you to the nearest cave biome. The Journey Begins. Each cave has its titan.

MACT III
ACT III · BOSS

Magnetron

WhereThe Magnetic Caves (magnetic steel). It assembles from the biome's debris.

FightIt magnetizes metal blocks and hurls them at you. Keep your distance, strike its exposed core.

LootThe Heart of Iron: key to the magnetic crafts.
TFACT III
ACT III · BOSS

The Forsaken

WhereThe Forlorn Hollows. If the Underzealots sacrifice a Vesper, the Forsaken rises.

FightMassive melee demon + ranged howls (Darkness). Light your arena, strike its flanks.

LootThe Pure Darkness to weave Shadow Silk: the armor of shadows.
LACT III
ACT III · BOSS

Licowitch

WhereThe Candy Cavity (candy cave). Climb her licorice tower.

FightThe dark chocolate witch summons Gingerbread Men. The easiest boss of the act.

LootThe Radiant Essence, The Secret Ingredient.
VBACT III
ACT III · BOSS

Void Blossom

WhereA garden-cave below the surface (glowing vines deep down).

Fight350 HP. Giant void flower: thorns, spores, hides between phases. Strike the heart when it opens; beware the blade-petals.

LootThe void materials (BoMD crafts).
WACT III
ACT III · BOSS

Warden

WhereThe Deep Dark and its Ancient Cities. Three Shrieker screams make it emerge.

FightIt is blind, hunts by sound. To kill it: bow from long range. Its sonic boom pierces walls. Or avoid it (sneak + snowballs).

LootThe Ancient City loot and the path to Dreadsteel gear.
NLACT III
ACT III · BOSS

Night Lich

WhereThe Lich Tower, in the frozen tundras. It appears at the top, at night.

Fight300 HP. Three phases: teleports, ice comets, phantom swarms. Destroy its soul stars. Regeneration and spectral arrows.

LootThe soul materials of BoMD.
TACT III
ACT III · BOSS

Tremorzilla

WhereThe radioactive Toxic Caves. Wear the full Hazmat set or the radiation kills you.

FightGodzilla: atomic ray, stomps. A very long war of attrition — or tame it (Nuclear Waste).

LootThe titan as a mount, and the nuclear materials.
LACT III
ACT III · BOSS

Luxtructosaurus

WhereThe Primordial Caves. Throw the Ominous Catalyst into the volcano crater: the undead dino rises.

FightThe hardest boss of Alex's Caves: meteors, bites, lava. Primordial armor + fire resistance REQUIRED.

LootIts remains forge the Extinction Spear, the Spear of Ages.
TAACT III
ACT III · BOSS

The Abyss: Hullbreaker

WhereThe Abyssal Chasm (underwater gorge). Repair a wreck submarine with copper and dive.

FightGiant abyssal serpent that crushes submarines. Flee it or fight it with the trident. The Mine Guardians explode.

LootThe Abyssal Ruins and trade with the Deep Ones: the items of Poseidon.
