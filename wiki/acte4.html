ACT IV · THE NETHER

THE BURNING SKIES

Wings blot the sun. Dragons remember.

The eternal flames. Forge netherite, face the fire lords. The Cataclysm bosses are located with an Eye you will craft here.

NMACT IV
ACT IV · BOSS

Netherite Monstrosity

WhereThe Soul Forge of the soul valleys. The Eye of Monstrous flies there like an eye of ender.

Fight600 HP. A tank of flesh and netherite: charges, shockwaves, lava geysers. It heals in lava. Strike its legs, dodge sideways. Fire resistance required.

LootThe Infernal Forge (gauntlet-hammer) and the Monstrous helmet: your first Cataclysm weapon.
NGACT IV
ACT IV · BOSS

Nether Gauntlet

WhereThe Gauntlet Arena, in the basalt deltas. The Blazing Eye guides you there.

Fight250 HP. Giant stone hand: it crushes you, throws you, fires lasers from its palm. Run in a circle, strike the fingers when it lands.

LootThe BoMD Nether materials.
IACT IV
ACT IV · BOSS

Ignis

WhereThe Burning Arena at the heart of the Nether. Find the Strange Key, open the Door of Seal, activate the altar.

Fight450 HP. THE technical duel. His soul shield protects him while guarding: strike ONLY when he attacks. Phase 2: blue sword, combos. The Epic Fight dodge is your best friend.

LootThe Ignitium (best fire armor) and the Burning Ashes to re-summon him.
WACT IV
ACT IV · BOSS

Wither

WhereYou summon it: 4 soul sand in a T + 3 wither skeleton skulls. Build it wherever YOU want.

FightWith the difficulty mods, it hits harder than vanilla. Golden apples, Smite V, take it down before it destroys everything.

LootThe Nether Star: beacon and crafts.
