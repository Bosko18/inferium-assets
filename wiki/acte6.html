LEGENDS NEVER MEANT TO WAKE

The old titans stir in their chains.

At the edge of the void, the dragons and the gates of the beyond.

EDACT VI
ACT VI · BOSS

Ender Dragon + Trigon

WhereThe End, via the Stronghold portal. This pack adds Baby Ender Dragons (Ender Trigon).

FightDestroy the crystals with the bow first, while the baby dragons harass you. Explosive beds are still allowed.

LootThe dragon egg and access to the outer islands.
OACT VI
ACT VI · BOSS

Obsidilith

WhereIts arena on the outer islands of the End (explore after the dragon).

Fight300 HP. Living obsidian pillar: teleports, trap pillars, runic salvos. Destroy the ground runes, strike between its phases.

LootThe obsidian heart (BoMD End crafts).
EGACT VI
ACT VI · BOSS

Ender Guardian

WhereThe Ruined Citadel of the outer islands. The Eye of Void locates it.

Fight333 HP. Colossal armored shulker-guardian: homing fists, gravity waves, closing arena. Strike its head when the armor opens. Ender pearls to dodge.

LootThe Gauntlet of Bulwark and the citadel's treasure.
VWACT VI
ACT VI · BOSS

Void Worm

WhereThe void between the End islands: it swims in nothingness. Glide on elytra, it will come.

FightDimensional serpent: it dives into portals and emerges elsewhere. Strike THE HEAD — the body absorbs almost everything.

LootThe means to cut space itself.
THACT VI
ACT VI · BOSS

The Harbinger

WhereThe buried Ancient Factory. The Eye of Mech locates it; wake the machine.

Fight390 HP. Flying war machine: death lasers, wither missiles, drones. Crossbow + cover. Its grounded phase is your damage window.

LootThe Witherite: a notch above netherite.
ARACT VI
ACT VI · BOSS

Ancient Remnant

WhereThe Cursed Pyramid of the deserts. The Eye of Desert locates it. Brush the suspicious sand. Beware the Wadjet serpent.

Fight450 HP. Mummified pharaoh-beast: sandstorms, charges, line quakes. Stay mobile, heal during its summons.

LootThe Ancient Spear and the Remnant's skull.
MACT VI
ACT VI · BOSS

Maledictus

WhereThe Frosted Prison of the polar lands. The Eye of Curse locates it. Aptrgangr and elite Draugr guard the prison; summon the cursed king at the altar.

Fight420 HP. THE peak of Cataclysm: phantom halberd, dive bombs, phantom swarms, curses. All your previous weapons serve HERE.

LootIts Phantom Halberd and the title of Cataclysmfarer.
