ACT V · OCEANS & STORMS

THE ABYSS CALLS

Oceans, voids and things without names.

The abysses and the skies. Titans of the seas and creatures of the storm.

TMACT V
ACT V · BOSS

The Myths of the Sea

WhereThe deep oceans hide five legends: Kraken, Leviathan, Abaia, Bake Kujira, Bunyip. Wear Scuba Gear and Tide Guardian armor before diving.

FightTrident Channeling or Riptide. Each creature has its territory; the Bake Kujira (ghost whale) is an omen as much as an opponent.

LootThe trophies of the seas.
TLACT V
ACT V · BOSS

The Leviathan

WhereThe Sunken City at the bottom of the oceans. The Eye of Abyss locates it; wake it at the altar.

Fight400 HP. INVINCIBLE out of water: fight while diving. Abyss beam that tracks you, tentacles, a bite that drags you. Scuba Gear + Depth Strider III. Break the beam's line of sight.

LootThe Tidal Claws: the grapple-claws of the abyss.
STACT V
ACT V · BOSS

Scylla, the Storm Empress

WhereThe Acropolis, a Greek city suspended IN THE SKY. The Eye of Storm locates it. Keep the Paraglider for the descent.

Fight390 HP. Chain lightning, bolt spears, and her Storm Serpent that hunts you. Platforms + dodge: NEVER stand still.

LootThe Ceraunus, the lightning lance.
